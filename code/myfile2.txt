The road ahead curved gently to the left and disappeared into a dense forest. 
The trees there were tall and ancient, their trunks thick as houses, 
their branches forming a canopy so dense that only thin slivers of light could filter through. 
He had heard stories about that forest how travelers sometimes got lost in it for days, 
wandering in circles until they finally stumbled out on the other side, shaken and disoriented but somehow changed, 
as if the forest had whispered something into their souls that they could not fully understand but could never quite forget.
